Creamy Kimchi Tteokbokki

Ingredients:

  • Kimchi
  • Sesame oil
  • Water
  • Gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin (sweet cooking rice wine)
  • Rice vinegar
  • Rice cakes (tteok)
  • Tofu
  • Cornstarch slurry (cornstarch mixed with half and half)
  • Cheese (type not specified, but for topping)
  • Black sesame seeds
